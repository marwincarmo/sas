4.1 Detecting redundancy with Unique Variable Analysis

redund <- EGAnet::UVA(data=items[,2:7], method="wTO", type="threshold",  corr =  "spearman",
                    reduce=FALSE, adhoc=FALSE, plot.redundancy=TRUE, reduce.method = "remove") 
redund.items <- redund$redundancy$redundant

High redundancies were found between items sas_3 (“The thought of going to sleep makes me uneasy.”) and sas_4, sas_6 (“I am scared of not being able to fall asleep.”).
